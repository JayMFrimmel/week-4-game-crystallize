Crystal & Gem Collector

How to Play:

  1. You will be given a random number at the start of each round of play.
  2. There are four crystals and gems pictured below. By clicking on a crystal/gem a specific amount of points will be revealed. The points will be added to your score in the point-keeping box.
  3. You win the game by matching your total score to the random number. You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.
  4. The value of each crystal/gem is hidden from you until you click on it.
  5. Each time the game starts, the game will give you new values for each crystal

Good Luck!
